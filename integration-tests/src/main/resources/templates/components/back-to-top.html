Back to top

PatternFly v6 back to top — a sticky button that appears after scrolling and smoothly returns to the top of the page.

Basic

The back-to-top button is hidden at the top of the page and appears once you scroll past 400px. Scroll down to see it appear in the bottom-right corner.

Always visible

This variant is always visible regardless of scroll position. It is shown here in a relative container so it does not compete with the real sticky button.
